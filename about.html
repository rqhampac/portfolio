about me

I am a Certified Public Accountant in the Philippines, a Certified Quickbooks Online ProAdvisor, a Certified Xero Advisor and a self-taught software developer with decades of experience in accounting, auditing and software development. I have a lot of experiences in accounting and ERP systems such as SAP B1, Exact Globe, Quickbooks and Xero.

The following are my bookkeeping services using Quickbooks Online and Xero:

  • Data migration.
  • Company setup.
  • Chart of Accounts setup.
  • Supplier,customer and inventory setup.
  • Recording of Invoice, bill, expenses and fixed assets.
  • Recording of bank, credit card and loan transactions.
  • Bank, credit card and loan reconciliations.
  • Accounts receivable/payable summary reports.
  • Profit and loss report.
  • Balance sheet report.
  • Cash flow report.

I provide free audit or consultation.

With my background in accounting, I was able to create payroll systems, inventory systems, product costing and financial reporting systems as well as payroll system. I am proficient in C#, .Net, MS Sql, Crystal Report, Dapper, Squirrel, HTML, CSS, Javascript and Angular. The tools that I use are Microsoft Visual Studio and Visual Studio Code.
